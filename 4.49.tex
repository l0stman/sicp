We just redefine \lstinline!parse-word! as follows in the grammar
defined in \textbf{4.48}.y

\begin{lstlisting}
(define (parse-word word-list)
  (list (car word-list)
        (an-element-of (cdr word-list))))
\end{lstlisting}
