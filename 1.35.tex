We have,
\[1 + \frac{1}{\phi} = \frac{\phi + 1}{\phi} =
 \frac{\phi^2}{\phi} =
 \phi\]
We could then define the golden ratio as
\begin{lstlisting}
(define phi
  (fixed-point (lambda (x) (+ 1 (/ 1 x)))
	       1.0))
\end{lstlisting}
